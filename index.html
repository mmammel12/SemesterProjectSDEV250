Collatz Conjecture

Start with any positive integer n. If the integer is even, divide it by 2. If the integer is odd multiply it by 3 then add 1. Repeat until the sequence reaches 1.

The conjecture states that all positive integers will eventually reach 1.

Current mathematics has not been able to prove or disprove this conjecture. However, all integers tested have reached 1.

 Enter an integer to see how many steps it takes to reach 1.
